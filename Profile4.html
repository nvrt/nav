Navreet Vaidwan

Executive Summary

A strong leader with over twenty-five years of experience in the areas of Internal Audit, Operational Risk, Enterprise Risk Management, Project Management, Resource Management, Activity Based Management, Business Process Improvement, International Trade, and Petroleum Refining. Navreet has worked in the Financial Services, Manufacturing, Telecommunications, Petroleum, Distribution, Retail and Utilities industries.
